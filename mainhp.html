Water Conservation Tips

Learn how to conserve water in your daily life. Simple steps include:

  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • Fix leaky faucets.
  • Use a broom instead of hosing down driveways.

Water Pollution Facts

Water pollution is a serious environmental issue. Here are some facts:

  • Over 80% of wastewater flows back into the ecosystem without being treated or reused.
  • Plastic waste is a significant contributor to water pollution.
  • One in every 10 people lacks access to clean water.
